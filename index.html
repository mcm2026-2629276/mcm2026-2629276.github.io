The Methodology

How we model the
energy flow.

Batteries aren't just percentages; they are complex chemical systems. Our model decomposes the smartphone into modular subsystems, calculating the Joule heating and current drain through a coupled ODE loop.

Hardware Decomposition

Screen (brightness-aware), Processor (utilization-coupled), Modem, and Thermal modules.

Thermal Feedback Loop

Accounts for internal resistance increases as the device gets hotter durring intensive tasks.

Governing ODEs

STATE OF CHARGE DECAY $$ \frac{ds}{dt} = -\frac{P_{total}(t, T)}{V_{cell} \cdot C_{nom}} $$
THERMAL EVOLUTION $$ m C_p \frac{dT}{dt} = P_{total} - \frac{T - T_{amb}}{R_{th}} $$
